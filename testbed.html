The gCIO Program is a joint project of the Career Executive Service Board (CESB), De La Salle University – College of Computer Studies (DLSU-CCS) and ideacorp. It provides real-world applicable knowledge combined with leading-edge thinking to address 21st century governance challenges.

We Enable the Philippine government to use Information and Communications Technology for citizen empowerment, good governance and sustainable development.

The course was designed with government, civil society, industry and academic partners. Faculties are also carefully selected to ensure that they have the experience and expertise. Cases and discussions are highly contextualized for the Philippine government to make it easy to apply what they learn as they return to work.

Global best-practices and standards are carefully and critically examined to build on learner’s knowledge and understanding of relevant principles. But more importantly, various training strategies are used to build the participant’s capacity to be effective gCIOs.

Modules for Health Informatics:

  • Module 1: e-Government & Leadership
  • Module 2: Government Enterprise Architecture
  • Module 3: Technology Life Cycle Management
  • Module 4: Communications Strategy
